WHO WE ARE

Columbia University Lion Dance (CULD) is a student-run group founded in the fall of 2000 with the help of the Columbia University Chinese Students Club (CSC) in New York City. Our style of lion dancing was first shaped by Tony Chuy’s Northern Mantis School & carries influences of Hung Gar & Southern Mantis foundations from contributions by our alumni.

JOIN CULD

In 2009, CULD became recognized at Columbia by ABC (Activities Board at Columbia). We are open to all, & no experience is necessary! We will teach you everything from techniques & tricks to history and customs, & we provide all equipment. We perform all over New York City as well as at Columbia for all sorts of occasions: campus events, corporate events, charity events, weddings, birthday parties, Chinese New Year events, you name it! We practice hard, have fun, & bring you a slice of traditional Chinese culture.
